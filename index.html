Industrial Revolution

[Factory/Industry Image]

The Industrial Revolution (18th–19th century) transformed the UK:

  • Steam engines and mechanized factories
  • Rapid urbanization and growth of cities
  • Transportation revolution (railways, canals)
  • Major social and economic changes

Fact: By 1850, Britain produced 40% of the world’s coal and iron!

Social Changes

The Industrial Revolution brought:

  • New working conditions and labor movements
  • Rise of the middle class
  • Urban poverty and public health reforms

Modern UK Politics

The UK today:

  • Constitutional monarchy with a parliamentary democracy
  • Prime Minister leads the government
  • Queen/King serves as ceremonial head of state
  • Member of the Commonwealth

Modern Economy

The UK has a strong global economy:

  • Financial hub: London
  • Advanced services and technology sectors
  • Major exports include machinery, aerospace, and pharmaceuticals

Society & Culture

Modern UK is diverse and culturally rich:

  • Multicultural population
  • Strong traditions in literature, music, and art
  • Major sports: Football, Rugby, Cricket

Contemporary issues:
